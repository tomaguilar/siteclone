Welcome to Arcane Streaming

A new music streaming service that plays non-mainstream music. Where up and coming artists share their music without competing against popular artists

For indie musicians, Arcane is a platform for receiving more exposure and expanding your fanbase beyond the limitations of your local area. While other apps/sites are focused on selling subscriptions to already popular artists, Arcane prides itself on being a community of rising artists and avid listeners.

Featured Artist

Quinn Kirkham

Quinn Kirkham, musician and composer, writes a wide variety of music, from easy listening to electronic and new age. His first album was "Instill" back in 2018. He is also known for his single "Electric Rain". Quinn Kirkham collaberates with others, one being his old roommate. They stayed up all night writing the song, "Emerald Throne". Despite having written over 300 songs, Quinn Kirkham is a perfectionist and pushes himself to a higher standard.
